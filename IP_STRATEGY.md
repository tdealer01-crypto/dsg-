# DSG Intellectual Property (IP) Strategy

To achieve the goal of 10,000+ GitHub stars while maintaining a viable business model, DSG employs a multi-layered IP strategy.

## 1. Copyright & Licensing (The Open Core)
- **The Core Engine**: Distributed under the **MIT License**. This is the "Growth Engine." It allows developers to use, modify, and distribute DSG freely, which is essential for viral adoption.
- **The Expression**: The specific implementation of the DSG algorithm in this repository is protected by copyright.

## 2. Trademark Protection (The Brand)
- **DSG™ & Deterministic Security Gate™**: We protect the brand name. While anyone can use the code, they cannot call their modified version "Official DSG" without permission. This ensures that as DSG becomes a standard, the original authors maintain authority.

## 3. Trade Secrets (The Revenue)
- **Enterprise Invariants**: The specific "Noble Invariants" used for high-stakes industries (e.g., SWIFT banking, Nuclear safety) are kept proprietary.
- **Hardware Acceleration Logic**: Optimized implementations for FPGA or ASIC are not open-sourced.

## 4. Patent Strategy (Defensive)
- While pure mathematical algorithms are difficult to patent, specific **system-level implementations** of DSG (e.g., "A method for deterministic state transition guarding in distributed ledger systems") can be filed as defensive patents to prevent patent trolls from blocking the project.

## 5. Open-Core Business Model
- **Community Edition (MIT)**: Full core functionality, open for all.
- **Enterprise Edition (Proprietary)**: Advanced features, compliance reporting, and specialized invariants.

---

*By combining Open Source for growth and Proprietary IP for value, DSG creates a sustainable ecosystem.*
